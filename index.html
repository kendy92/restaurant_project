The Flavours of Saffron

Bringing in the exotic flavours from the plains of North India to the 6ix, Saffron Restaurant strives to be a one-stop shop for all you foodies. Here, the traditionally rich north-west frontier food meets modern international art of cooking. Apart from the delicious butter chicken, dal makhni, biryani, we have seasonal cuisines which will delight your taste buds and leave you asking for more! We are currently located in Downtown, Markham and Etobicoke, with 2 more locations in the making.
