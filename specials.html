Daily Specials

Are you up for trying something new? Something with an extra zing?

Each day at Saffron Restaurant, we offer a special cuisine embellished with the essence of asian spices, herbs and above all ... LOVE. Try out are daily specials with your friends and family in an exquisite Indo-Canadian environment.

Kashmiri Rogan Josh

$15.25

Break the monotony of Monday with our signature dish from the North, Kashmiri Rogan Josh. Robust with varied spices, the dish is cooked with the aroma of fennel seeds, garam masala, bay leaves, turmeric and is indeed a celebration of all senses.

Punjabi Butter Chicken

$18.35

On Tuesday, gorge on this amazing plate of buttery heaven. Chunks of chicken marinated overnight in yogurt and a beautiful mix of spices, served with a dollop of melting cream or butter on top.

Banjari Gosht

$16.75

Up for something entirely new? Every Wednesday, experience the authentic flavours of Rajasthani spices, infused in juicy mutton pieces. Dive deep in a foodie euphoria with this exquisite blend of yogurt and juicy mutton pieces.

Kakori Kabab

$13.85

A famous Nawabi recipe from Lucknow passed down through generations. It derives its name from the city of Kakori on the outskirts of Lucknow. It is made with the finest meat of the lamb and a few spices.

Hyderabadi Biryani

$19.55

Get crazy on Fridays with everyone's favourite mughlai dish - Hyderabadi Biryani. It's the perfect choice for foodies who prefer having their rice with spice, interspersed with tender peaces of meat or chicken. Cooked in 'dum style', it's layered with fried onions and mint, served with a cold raita.

Dilli Murgh Jalfrezi

$17.25

From the street flavours of Delhi-6, enjoy this delightfully flavorful curry with tender juicy chunks of chicken in a spicy tomato sauce studded with stir-fried peppers and onions.

Malai Kofta

$11.25

End the week with the most monumental vegetarian dish of North India. The koftas are made with a mix of potatoes, carrots, beans, peas, and sweet corn, tantalising your taste buds like no other vegetarian dish ever!
